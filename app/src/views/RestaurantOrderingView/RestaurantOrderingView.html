Order Food
SCENARIO
In this demo we explore a version of how to order food at a burger restaurant - without touching the screen. This scenario could also apply to other purchasing experiences, and can be build out to have sub-categories of items etc. depending on the restaurants offerings.
HOW TO
Put your hand over the product you want to buy - spread your fingers to select.
Checkout by holding your hand over the checkout button and spread your fingers.
